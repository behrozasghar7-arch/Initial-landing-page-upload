FAQs

Honest answers, no fluff.

The things people usually ask before we start.

What do I need to start? +
Your LinkedIn profile link, brand colours if you have them, your offer and any inspiration you like.
How long does it take? +
Most small projects take a few days. Full branding projects may take longer depending on the scope.
Can you design only a LinkedIn banner? +
Yes, you can start with only a banner if you do not need the full profile branding.
Do you provide editable files? +
Yes, Canva or design source files can be provided depending on the package.
Is this for beginners? +
Yes. I keep the process simple and guide you step by step.
Do you write the content too? +
I can help improve the messaging and structure, but the main focus is design and branding.
